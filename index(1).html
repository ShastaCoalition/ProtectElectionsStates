📞 What to Say When You Call
Keep it under 60 seconds. Say your name, your city, and one clear ask. Staff will ask your ZIP code — have it ready.
🏛️ For Your Governor
"Hi, my name is [NAME] and I'm a constituent in [CITY]. I'm calling to demand [GOVERNOR] immediately issue executive orders protecting [STATE]'s elections — starting with the June 2 primary and through the November midterms: a state-enforced protection perimeter around all polling places, a ban on surrendering voter data to federal agencies without a court order, and deployment of the State National Guard to protect ballot counting centers. These orders take days to issue — we need them now."

📋 Three Essential State Laws to Demand
Demand your state legislators introduce and pass these three protections with urgency designation — so they take effect before November.
🚫
Ban 3rd-Party Voter Challenges
Prohibit private groups and the DOJ from using digital spreadsheets to mass-challenge voter registrations. No voter should have to justify their right to vote to a stranger. Stops right-wing mass-purges dead.
⛔
Prohibit Voter Intimidation
Establish broad constitutional protections with severe criminal penalties and civil remedies — including injunctions and monetary damages — for those who threaten voters or election workers.
✅
Close the Certification Loophole
Certification is a mandatory math and clerical step — not a judgment call, not optional, not subject to personal opinion. The numbers are counted. The law requires the signature. Local judges must have the power to compel certification on the spot. Officials who refuse face immediate civil and criminal liability.
